What the H*ck: A Self-Censorship Tool.

Tired of wondering whether your papers, grant proposals, or documentation will be flagged for “unacceptable” language? What the Heck dot Tech is a lightweight, browser-based tool that instantly scans any block of text against the evolving list of over 250 words and phrases being purged from U.S. government documents; terms ranging from “climate change” and “abortion” to "female,” "vaccines,” and “Gulf of Mexico!”
