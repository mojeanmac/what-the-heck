What the Heck: A Self-Censorship Tool!

Tired of wondering whether your papers, grant proposals, or documentation will be flagged for “unacceptable” language? What the Heck dot Tech is a lightweight, browser-based tool that instantly scans any block of text against the ever-evolving list of more than 250 words and phrases reportedly being purged from U.S. government documents; terms ranging from “climate change” and “abortion” to “Native American,” “disability,” and “Gulf of Mexico.”
